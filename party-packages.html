FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book a package? ▼

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for party packages, and 4+ weeks for the food truck. Peak season (May–August, November–December) books fast — the earlier the better!

Do you deliver? What's your service area? ▼

Yes! We deliver throughout Maryland and select DMV areas. Delivery fees vary by distance. The food truck travels within a reasonable radius — contact us with your venue address for availability.

Can I customize any package? ▼

Absolutely! Every package is fully customizable — flavors, themes, colors, dietary needs. Just tell us what you're envisioning in your consultation and we'll make it work.

What's the deposit and payment process? ▼

We require a 50% deposit to lock in your date. The remaining balance is due at pickup or delivery. We accept all major payment methods.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? ▼

We do our best to accommodate gluten-free, nut-free, and other dietary needs. Please let us know during the consultation so we can plan accordingly. We cannot guarantee allergen-free environments.
