Urgent Issues — Fix These First

🔴 No Google Business Profile / Reviews

When someone searches "custom cakes Maryland" or "birthday cake Westminster MD" right now — Sweet Konfections doesn't appear. Google is where buying decisions happen. This is your #1 most urgent fix.

→ Fix: Set up Google Business Profile, verify your location, add photos, and ask your last 10 customers to leave a review. Takes 1 week, pays off for years.

🔴 Facebook Profile Is Incomplete — 138 Followers

No cover photo, no call-to-action button set up, no regular posting schedule. Customers who find you on Facebook see an incomplete profile and move on. This is losing you sales every day.

→ Fix: Add a professional cover photo (food truck or cake spread), set CTA button to "Order Now" linking to your website, and start a consistent posting schedule. We handle all of this for you.

🔴 Bakesy Is Taking a Cut of Every Order

Bakesy is a third-party platform — you're building your customer base on a storefront you don't own. If they change their pricing, add fees, or shut down, your ordering system disappears with it. A direct order form on your own website means you own the relationship with your customers completely.

→ Fix: Your new website includes a direct order form — free, no platform fees. Every dollar from every order goes straight to you.

🔴 No Customer Email or Text List

You're doing 6+ festivals a year with hundreds of people trying your food. An automated email or text follow-up system means every festival buyer can be reached again for new items, upcoming events, and holiday specials — turning one-time buyers into repeat customers automatically.

→ Fix: Add a simple QR code sign at your booth: "Text us for exclusive deals + event alerts." We set this up and automate the follow-up messages so you don't have to do anything.

🟡 Website Can't Be Updated by You

The current GoDaddy site requires a developer or tech knowledge to change anything — prices, photos, events, menu items. Every time something changes, you either pay someone or leave outdated info online.

→ Fix: Your new WordPress + Elementor site lets you click and type to update anything yourself in 2 minutes — no coding, no developer, no fees.

At your next festival, put a small sign at the booth: "Want to know where we'll be next? Scan for event alerts + exclusive deals." Every person who scans gets added to your text/email list and receives automated follow-up messages. That's how a one-time festival buyer becomes a repeat customer.

🏢 Corporate & Event Catering — High Ticket Orders

Companies in the Maryland and DC area regularly need desserts for office parties, corporate events, and team celebrations. A single corporate order can be $300–$1,000+. With a proper website and online presence, you can target this market with a single page and LinkedIn outreach.

📸 Instagram Reels of Your Process = Free Viral Marketing

Short videos of cake decorating, funnel cake fries being made at your truck, or cookie dipping consistently go viral in the food niche. One 30-second video can reach 10,000–100,000 people at no cost. Your products are visually stunning — this is one of the highest-ROI moves available to you right now.

→ Estimated impact: 200–500 new Instagram followers per viral Reel, 10–30 new inquiries
