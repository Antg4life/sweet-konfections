Managing Your Site

How to Update Your Website

WordPress + Elementor makes updates as easy as clicking and typing. No coding, no tech skills needed.

1

Log into your WordPress Dashboard

Go to sweetkonfections.com/wp-admin and enter your username and password. You'll land on the main dashboard.

Bookmark this page on your phone and computer
2

Open the page you want to edit

In the left menu click Pages, find the page you want to change (Home, Treats, Gallery, etc.), and click Edit with Elementor.

3

Click anything to edit it

In Elementor, just click on any text to change the words, or click an image to swap it out. Everything is drag-and-drop.

Changes are live only after you click the green "Publish" button
4

Save and publish

When you're done, click the green Publish button at the bottom left of the Elementor panel. Your changes go live immediately.

Quick Reference

Most Common Updates

These are the things you'll update most often. Each one takes less than 2 minutes.

🎂 Update a Price

Go to Pages → find the product page → Edit with Elementor → click the price text → type the new price → Publish.

Pages → Treats (or Cakes) → Edit → click price → update → Publish

📸 Swap a Photo

Click the image in Elementor → click the photo icon → upload your new photo from your phone or computer → Publish.

Click image → upload new photo → Publish

🚚 Add a New Event

Go to your Homepage → Find Our Truck section → click an existing event card → duplicate it → update the date, name, and location → Publish.

Home → Find Our Truck → duplicate card → edit → Publish

🖼️ Add Gallery Photos

Go to Pages → Gallery → Edit with Elementor → click any gallery item → upload new photo → update the label → Publish.

Pages → Gallery → click photo → upload → Publish

📝 Update Your About Section

Go to Pages → Home → Edit with Elementor → scroll to the "Our Story" section → click the text → edit → Publish.

Home → Our Story section → click text → edit → Publish

📦 Add a New Product

Go to the relevant page → Edit with Elementor → right-click an existing product card → Duplicate → update the photo, name, description, and price → Publish.

Product page → right-click card → Duplicate → edit → Publish

Action Items

5 Things to Do in Your First Week

These quick steps will make your site feel completely yours and help customers find you faster.

1

Upload your best cake photos to the Gallery

The gallery is the first thing new customers explore. Add your most recent custom cakes, cupcakes, and food truck shots from Instagram. Aim for 20–30 real photos.

2

Add your next 3 upcoming events to "Find Our Truck"

Customers love knowing where to find you. Keeping this section updated drives real foot traffic to your booth and truck.

3

Share your new website link on Instagram and Facebook

Post: "We just launched our new website! 🎂 Visit sweetkonfections.com to browse our menu, view the gallery, and place your order." — and add the link to your Instagram bio.

4

Update your Facebook page profile and cover photo

A complete Facebook profile builds trust and helps customers find you in search. Add a clear profile photo (your logo or a great product shot) and a cover photo showing your truck or a beautiful cake spread.

This takes 5 minutes and makes a huge first impression
5

Ask your 5 most recent customers to leave a Google review

Text them directly: "Hey! I just launched a new website — would you mind leaving a quick Google review for Sweet Konfections? It really helps! 🙏" Five new reviews can double your local search visibility.
