Revenue Growth Strategy

10 Creative Ways We Can Help Sweet Konfections Sell More Sweets

Beyond the website — here are specific strategies we can implement to grow your revenue, reach new customers, and build loyalty with existing ones.

Social Media

Weekly "Behind the Bake" Reels

Short TikTok/Instagram Reels showing cake decorating, treat-making, and food truck prep. Behind-the-scenes content gets 3–5x more reach than product photos alone.

Seasonal Revenue

Holiday Pre-Order Campaigns

Automated email/SMS campaigns before Mother's Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Valentine's Day. Capture early orders and avoid last-minute chaos with a pre-order window.

Corporate Sales

B2B Dessert Packages for Offices

Monthly dessert subscriptions for local businesses — office parties, employee appreciation, client gifting. One corporate client can equal 12 monthly orders on autopilot.

Festival Strategy

Turn Festival Foot Traffic Into Bookings

You're already at AFRAM, Moonewell, Hentastic Harvest, wine festivals, and more. A QR code at the truck + a simple SMS opt-in captures every visitor. That festival crowd becomes your private event pipeline — and makes every run more profitable.

Referral Growth

"Share & Save" Referral Program

Give existing customers a discount code to share with friends. Every referral gets tracked automatically — no manual work for you. Word-of-mouth is your strongest channel.

Event Bundling

Wedding Vendor Partner Network

Partner with local wedding venues, photographers, and planners to be their preferred dessert vendor. Each partner sends you a steady stream of qualified leads.

Digital Menu

QR Menu Cards for the Food Truck

Printed QR cards at the truck that take customers straight to your website to book the truck for their own event. Turn every food truck customer into a future booking.

Customer Retention

Birthday & Anniversary Reminders

Collect customer birthdays at checkout. Automated emails go out 2 weeks before their birthday with a special discount. Turns one-time buyers into yearly customers.
